How it works

In poker tournaments, blind structures are essential to ensure that the game progresses in an even manner towards a winner.

Poker Blinds Timer takes care of all the maths with its in-built calculator, so you can sit back and relax.

Poker Blinds Timer works out the optimal blind increment based on starting stack size, desired length of game, number of players, timing of breaks, break length, and will keep track of all aspects of the blinds, so you finish on time, stress-free.

The app does all the work, so you can sit back and play.
